Monevo Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 31, 2025

Welcome to Monevo, an expense tracking mobile application developed to help users manage and visualize their financial spending.

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our app.

1. Information We Collect

a. Personal Information

  • Email address (for authentication and account recovery)
  • Basic profile information (if provided voluntarily)

b. Usage Data

  • App usage statistics (e.g., screens visited, time spent)
  • Device information (device type, OS version)
  • Crash reports and performance analytics

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide secure login and user authentication
  • Store and sync your expense data
  • Improve app performance and user experience
  • Respond to user support requests
  • Maintain and enhance app security

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is securely stored using Google Firebase, which follows strict data protection and encryption standards. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties. All communication between the app and Firebase is encrypted via HTTPS.

4. Third-Party Services

Monevo uses third-party services that may collect information used to identify you. These include:

  • Firebase Authentication
  • Firebase Firestore / Realtime Database
  • Firebase Analytics

Each service follows its own privacy policies, available at:
https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy
